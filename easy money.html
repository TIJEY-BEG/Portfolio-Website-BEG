Overview:

Money is not all, but important for our everyday life. If you plan a wedding Party,
to travel the world, or desire to purchase a big house: You need the required cash! Most People
struggle to save Money. They live in the "here and now", and prefer to act out their lives.

Objective:

Easy Money is a simple to use app, everyone can Benefit from. The target audience are mainly young people,
but also older age bracklets. The user can create saving Goals, watch the progress of his goals, and while he keeps
track of everything, images can be added to visualize the user's motivation and him/her why not to give up.

Design process:

  1. Project brief was given

  2. Three user flows were chosen from the brief

  3. One user flow/story was selected and mapped

  4. Quick sketches on paper for the first wireframes

  5. Sketches were turned into digital low fidelity wireframes

  6. Middle fidelity wireframes based on the pattern of low-fidelity wireframes

  7. User testing: A prototype test to see how users
    interact with the mobile screens of middle fidelity wireframes.
    According to user feedback I applied adjustments when I developed
    the high fidelity screens.

  8. Finishing screens based on user feedback

  9. As requested after that, desktop screens added

  10. Desktop screens designed without wireframing
    The idea was to keep desktop screens simple and in the
    same style like the mobile screens, add the main user flow
    of creating a saving goal, and to add functions like stats
    and links to different news websites.
