Speed Learning is a flashcards learning app. You can create your very own stacks and Cards, to learn whatever you want and the way you like. It includes different features to add audio
files or Images to your cards. You can also download stacks from other users out of the Cloud, edit These stacks for your own use, or publish your stacks to share them with other users.
